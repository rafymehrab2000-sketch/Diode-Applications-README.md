# Calculations

Supply Voltage = 12 V  
Load Resistor = 1 kΩ  

Using Ohm’s Law:

I = V / R

I = 12 / 1000  
I = 0.012 A  
I = 12 mA

The diode must support at least 12 mA forward current.

Selected diode: 1N4007

Maximum forward current rating = 1 A  
This is much greater than 12 mA, so the diode can safely handle the required current.

Maximum reverse voltage = 1000 V  
This is far greater than the 12 V supply, so it provides a large safety margin.
